The Dead Beat Poets

A Collection of Souls

Sab be-asar ho gaya hai aaj kal, na dawa ka asar bacha, na dua ka, jo dard tere khayalon se uthta hai, woh na likhne se rukta hai, na kehne se thakta hai. Sab be-asar… teri khamoshi bhi shor macha jaati hai, aur meri har baat tere ek "theek hoon" ke aage be-jaan si lagti hai. Maine har tareeqa aazma liya, khud ko samjhana, khud se ladna, raat ko hansna, aur din me chup-chup ke bikharna… par tera ek ehsaas sab kuchh be-asar kar deta hai. Kuchh zakhm theek hone ke liye bane hi nahi hote.
