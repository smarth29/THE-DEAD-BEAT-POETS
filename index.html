The Dead Beat Poets

Sab be-asar ho gaya hai aaj kal, na dawa ka asar bacha, na dua ka, jo dard tere khayalon se uthta hai, woh na likhne se rukta hai, na kehne se thakta hai. Sab be-asar… teri khamoshi bhi shor macha jaati hai, aur meri har baat tere ek “theek hoon” ke aage be-jaan si lagti hai. Maine har tareeqa aazma liya, khud ko samjhana, khud se ladna, raat ko hansna, aur din me chup-chup ke bikharna… par tera ek ehsaas sab kuchh be-asar kar deta hai. Sab be-asar ho gaya hai, waqt ka guzarna, logon ka samjhana, aur yeh kehna ki “sab theek ho jaayega.” Kuchh zakhm theek hone ke liye bane hi nahi hote. Mujhe nahi chahiye koi ilaaj, koi tasalli, bas itna sa chahiye ki tu ek baar samajh le— ki jab tu door hoti hai, toh meri saari koshishen sirf lafz reh jaati hain, aur main… main khud ke liye bhi sab be-asar ho jaata hoon.

Hum dono ke beech jo lafzon ka tufaan sa chal raha hai, Woh na teri zid ka natija hai, na meri jazbaaton ki gardish ka. Sach toh yeh hai ki hum dono ek hi kashthi ke musafir hain, Bas lehrein alag-alag taraf se takra rahi hain… aur hum dono ko dard samaan ka lag raha hai. Jo jhagde hum par lagte hain, Woh asal me hum vs duniya ki jung hai, Woh har woh baat jo humein todne ki koshish karti hai, Har woh pal jo humein ek dusre se door dhakelna chahta hai… Usi se ladte-ladte hum kabhi kabhi ek dusre ko hi teer bana dete hain. Par haqeeqat ye hai… Hum ek dusre ke khilaaf nahi, balki saath khade hain— Sirf zakhm alag-alag jagah lage hain. Tu bhi thak chuki hai, main bhi sookh chuka hoon, Tu rona chaahti hai, main chup reh kar sab seh leta hoon… Isliye lagta hai jaise ladai hum dono ki hai— Par jaan, hum dono toh ek hi paksh ke sipahi hain. Kabhi ruk kar sochna… Hamari baat aapas ki nahi hoti, Hamari ladai ek dusre se nahi hoti. Hamari ladai uss taqdeer se hoti hai Jo baar-baar humein parkhne aati hai. Aur agar kal kabhi koi pooche, “Kaun jeeta?” Hum muskurakar kehna— “Jeet humari thi… kyunki hum dono ek dusre ke saath khade the.”
